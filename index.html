About Us

We are a group of coding enthusiast students and professional mentors learning and sharing our knowledge of different technologies

  • Web Development is the major sector, we are active in
  • Open Source Contribution is somethiing we promote at our club
  • Junior Coders from high schools are being trained by our members

Technocrat Coders is an initiative taken by Khwab Foundation, Motihari and supported by LND College, Motihari to promote coding culture in the rural and backward regions of India. We share our learnings to encourage our upcoming generations about software & IT industry
